Ink Spirit

Write on the page, and the paper answers.

Ink Spirit is a sheet of paper that answers back: write any question with Apple Pencil, pause for a moment, and a handwritten reply slowly appears on the page.

FAQ

No reply after writing? Make sure your device is online. About 3 seconds after you stop writing, your ink dissolves into the paper and three breathing ink dots appear at the top — that means it is thinking.

How do I tear off the current page? Double-tap the page with two fingers.

How do I change the handwriting size or font? Tap the ink dot in the bottom-right corner to open settings.
