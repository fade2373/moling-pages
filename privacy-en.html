Ink Spirit — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

Ink Spirit (the "App") respects and protects your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

What we process

Your handwriting. When you finish writing on the page, your handwriting is rendered as an image and sent over an encrypted connection (HTTPS) to our server, which forwards it to Alibaba Cloud's Qwen large-language-model service to generate a reply. The image is used only to generate that single reply: our server does not store your handwriting, does not link it to any identity, and does not use it for model training or any other purpose.

Conversation context. To keep replies coherent, the text of your most recent exchanges is kept in your device's memory and sent along with the next request; it is cleared when the App exits. We do not store your conversation history on our servers.

What we do not collect

  • No account required — we do not collect your name, email, or phone number
  • No device identifiers for tracking, no advertising or analytics SDKs
  • No tracking of your activity across other apps or websites
  • What you write is never linked to who you are

Third-party services

Replies are generated by Alibaba Cloud's Qwen models. Your handwriting image is relayed through our server to Alibaba Cloud for processing, subject to its privacy and security commitments (Alibaba Cloud's Qwen service does not use API inputs to train models).

Children's privacy

The App is designed for users of all ages, collects no personally identifiable information, and applies child-appropriate safety constraints to generated content.

Your rights

Because we store none of your personal data, there is no server-side personal information to access, correct, or delete. For any privacy questions, contact: 2744467183@qq.com

Changes

Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
